About the Project

The Gates of Hell is a tower defense first-person shooter developed from 2021-2023, where I honed my game development skills through hands-on experimentation and iteration.

Players defend an unborn angel from waves of demonic enemies while fighting their way out of hell. The game features a unique three-mode system: Combat Mode for active FPS combat, Defense Mode for building walls and defensive structures around the protected object, and Tower Mode for placing specialized towers including turrets, healers, and resource printers.

This solo project served as my comprehensive game development training ground. I created 75-100+ hand-crafted 3D models including characters, environmental assets, weapons, and tower structures‚Äîall built from scratch in Blender. The project showcases my ability to execute the full development pipeline: game design, 3D modeling, C# programming, AI development, and audio production.

While the project was eventually shelved to pursue more advanced concepts, it represents a critical learning phase where I mastered core game development skills through iteration and problem-solving. The technical challenges I overcame‚Äîparticularly developing 4+ unique enemy AI systems including a sophisticated flying AI with raycasting-based decision making‚Äîlaid the foundation for my current game development expertise.
