About Me

I'm a junior at Stanford University studying Computer Science with a focus in Human-Computer Interaction, while competing as a varsity athlete on the Men's Volleyball team.

As co-founder and lead developer at BranchOff, I'm building an iOS app that helps parents manage their children's screen time more effectively. We're currently testing with over 100 families in California, with a public App Store launch coming soon.

Beyond professional development, I'm passionate about game development. I've participated in multiple game jams, created "The Gates of Hell" (a tower defense FPS), and I'm actively working on new game projects. I love exploring the intersection of compelling gameplay, narrative design, and technical innovation.
